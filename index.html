🐘 Interactive Widget

Meet Ellie,
your tiny elephant friend

A minimalist, classy bot that lives at the edge of your screen — click her and she'll walk all the way over to say hello.

↘ Look for Ellie in the corner!
🚶

Walks to you

Tap Ellie and she'll walk across the screen, swinging her trunk, all the way to the center to greet you.

👀

Peeks from edges

When idle, she sneaks to the nearest edge and peeks in, bobbing up and down — too cute to ignore.

🐾

Footprints

She leaves little footprints as she trots across the screen, creating a trail of her journey.

🎮

Mini-games

Rock Paper Scissors, trivia, jokes, and a mood tracker — all packed into the playground panel.

🖱️

Drag anywhere

Grab Ellie and drop her wherever you like — she stays put with a colorful particle trail.

💌

Drop-in embed

Two lines of HTML is all it takes to add Ellie to any existing webpage instantly.

📌 Add to any page

Paste inside the <body> tag of any HTML file:

<link rel="stylesheet" href="bot.css" />
<script src="bot.js" defer></script>

Make sure bot.css and bot.js are in the same folder (or update the paths).
